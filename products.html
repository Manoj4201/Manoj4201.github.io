Smart Physiological Monitoring System

This solution encompasses a device that detects a range of vital health parameters with the help of wearable sensors integrated into a single unit. The data collected is then transmitted to a central monitoring system.

This solution enables precise monitoring and transmission of vital parameters for further predictive analysis and to health care professionals for prescriptive analysis. As a continuous ambulatory monitoring device it detects ECG, heart rate, non-invasive blood pressure, SPO2 and GSR records physiological responses to real time events

Smart Pedometer

A wearable device that continuously monitors normal, aerobic steps and body movements The data collected is used to compute and present an overall picture of the total steps, Kilo calories and distance covered per day by the user. The data monitored can then be communicated to health care professionals for prescriptive analysis.
